General knowledge

Introduction

Nowadays, people underestimate the importance of contact with nature for our health. Shutting off the problems of everyday life allows you to regulate your stress levels. There is evidence of how our bodies deal with stress if we try to adapt to a potentially hostile environment such as cold water. Cold water swimming is also known as winter swimming, mainly during the winter and generally in the water below 16 degrees Celsius temperature. Best results can be achieved in temperature around 0 degrees Celsius.

Ice Man

The human body is highly complicated. It seems to us that we should focus a lot on negative stimuli and eliminate them from our lives. One such stimulation may be exposed to cold. As shown by famous people such as Wim Hof, who have successfully exposed their body to cold and unfavourable conditions for many years, our body can adapt to a new environment.

Data Collection Methodology
For this study, I use materials generally available on the Internet. Much of my work is based on materials found on the eCore search engine located on Moodle. The JURN.org website helped me build solid arguments and answer critical questions. During my research, I also used a top-rated personality such as Wim Hof - Global health leader, the Ice Man. He is an example of a person with incredible charisma and strength of character. Thanks to his knowledge, he can popularize healthy habits in society.
On the website www.wimhofmethod.com run by his team, you can find a lot of helpful knowledge. Scientists from Europe and the USA tested Wim Hof Radboud University, The Netherlands (2014) and Wayne State University Michigan, USA (2018)
Wim Hof approaches the subject of therapy more comprehensively. In addition to cold therapy, he also uses breathing methods that improve and increase the efficiency of his body. In my research, I want to focus only on the exposure of the body to cold and its effect on our body. Addressing the topic of breathing methods and showing scientific research is beyond the scope of my considerations.
